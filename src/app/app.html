Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your yoga journey with Ksenia

Do I need prior yoga experience?

▼
Not at all! My sessions are designed to meet you wherever you are in your practice. Whether you're a complete beginner or have years of experience, I'll tailor each session to your individual needs and comfort level.

What equipment do I need for virtual sessions?

▼
You'll need a yoga mat, comfortable clothing, and a quiet space with good internet connection. Optional props like blocks, straps, or blankets can enhance your practice, but I'll provide alternatives using household items if needed.

How are sessions structured?

▼
Each 60-minute session includes a gentle warm-up, breathing exercises, yoga postures tailored to your goals, relaxation, and a brief meditation. I also incorporate traditional Indian techniques and modern wellness practices.

Can I reschedule or cancel sessions?

▼
Yes, I understand life happens! Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Emergency situations are always handled with understanding and flexibility.

What makes your approach different?

▼
My training in India has given me deep insights into authentic yoga traditions. I combine ancient wisdom with modern understanding of anatomy and wellness, creating a holistic experience that addresses mind, body, and spirit.

Do you offer group sessions?

▼
Yes! I offer both 1:1 personalized sessions and group classes. Group sessions are a great way to connect with like-minded practitioners while enjoying the community aspect of yoga practice.
